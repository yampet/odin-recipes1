Ingredients

  • Ravioli pasta
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spinach
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Marinara sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • Fresh basil
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, spinach, Parmesan cheese, eggs, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  2. Roll out pasta dough and cut into squares.
  3. Place a spoonful of filling on each square and fold over to form ravioli.
  4. Seal edges with water and flour mixture.
  5. Boil ravioli in salted water until they float.
  6. In a skillet, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  7. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Drain ravioli and toss in the sauce.
  9. Serve with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
  10. Enjoy your homemade ravioli!
