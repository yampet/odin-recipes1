Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and onion until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  8. Top with remaining meat sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  10. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Notes

You can add vegetables like spinach or mushrooms for extra flavor.

For a vegetarian version, substitute ground beef with lentils or mushrooms.

Pair with garlic bread and a side salad for a complete meal.

Tips

  • Use no-boil lasagna noodles to save time.
  • Let the lasagna sit for a few minutes before cutting to help it hold its shape.
  • Make it ahead of time and refrigerate or freeze for later use.
  • Experiment with different cheeses like provolone or gouda for a unique flavor.
  • Use fresh herbs like basil or parsley for added freshness.
  • Cover with foil for the first 30 minutes of baking to prevent over-browning.
  • Drizzle with olive oil before serving for extra richness.
  • Serve with a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a spicy kick.
  • Pair with a glass of red wine for a classic Italian experience.
  • Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
  • Reheat in the oven for best results, or microwave for a quick meal.
